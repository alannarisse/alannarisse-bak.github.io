Design Checklist

Things to remember

  • Fonts should be no smaller than 14px. A body size of 20px is the most popular right now.
  • Line widths are easiest read at a max of 66 characters. This generally turns out to be about 500px wide for body copy. for content wider than 66 characters, consider adding a second column or give it a max-width on larger screens so it stays readable.
  • Stick to TWO FONTS ONLY. There's a reason they call it font pairing, not font menage-a-trois. I love this webpage for font pairing ideas.
  • S P A C E: Websites love it. Padd it up! It looks better and feels better.
